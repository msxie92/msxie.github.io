Biography

She is a Ph.D. candidate in Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. WONG, Tien-Tsin. Before that she received the B. Eng. degree in Software Engineering from South China University of Technology (SCUT) in 2015. She obtained the M.Phil. degree in Computer Science and Technology from the same university in 2018 under the supervision of Prof. XU, Xuemiao.
Her research interests cover Computer Graphics, Computer Vision and Deep Learning. Specifically, she is interested in facilitating manga production with deep learning technology.
